CUTC 2016: Imagine

Since 1999, CUTC (the Canadian Undergraduate Technology Conference) has sparked a passion for technology amongst 4500+ students across Canada.

At CUTC, you’ll see where the future of technology is headed, learn from the best, build things that matter and meet hustlers, makers and builders like you.

We’ll have 20+ passionate speakers sharing their success and failures, what technology means to them, and how you can make impact.

You’ll get to compete for $5000+ in prizes and challenges where you can learn by doing.

Above all, we’ll make sure you’ll have 1 unforgettable weekend.

Why Should I come?

PLEASE, FORGET EVERYTHING YOU’VE EVER LEARNT ABOUT HOW TO BEHAVE AT A CONFERENCE...

Snip your ties, fight for the microphone, and act like the true technology-driven genius students that you are. CUTC is a place where you no longer have to pretend, and where real technology innovators and entrepreneurs are university students – acting like a 30 year old isn’t buying you any street cred, so drop it.

Hackathons are great to learn and build, CUTC is where you connect with people on things you care about and kickstart friendships. We’ve structured our 16th CUTC to be a continuous zero-BS experience; you really won’t want to skip an event, even a single hour of it.

Our speakers are some of the hottest founders and inspirational creators that have set foot in Toronto. They want to interact with you and entertain your most controversial of questions... nobody wants to hear another “What inspires you to be the best?”. You’ll end Day 1 with a fast-paced AI Competition or a ridiculously intense Product Vision Challenge. You’ll be performing with the best, against the best, and with serious mentorship.

Day 2 is all about specialization and breakouts. Whether you’re a VR/wearable fanboy, a robot fanatic, a disciple of AI & machine learning, a nanotechnology geek, a product person, a code junkie, a designer dame, a startup buff, a security expert, a biotech padawan or a space enthusiast - we’ve got talks all across the board for the tech explorer in you.

Welcome to Imagine.

You!

Are you a student? Are you passionate about the future of technology and the impact it will have? Then CUTC Imagine is for you!

This year we’re opening up the floor to include high school and graduate students as well.

Who are we?

An organising team with a passion to make impact. Alumni who are inspired to innovate.

We are entrepreneurs: members of our community have gone on to start over 30 companies including Nulogy, Polar Mobile, ThinkPanda, Neverfrost, Kira Talent and more.

We are leaders: others have risen to leadership positions in companies such as Google, Microsoft, Facebook, BCG and others.

We are academics: more have gone on to complete MBAs & PhDs from world-renowned schools such as Harvard, INSEAD, John Hopkins, and Wharton.

This year, we’re back to provide you the best place to indulge in all things tech.

FAQ

When and where is CUTC Imagine?

CUTC will be held on May 7-8th at the MaRS Innovation District in the heart of downtown Toronto.

What’s different at CUTC then say, at a hackathon?

At CUTC, it’s not just about hacking. Sure, you can get your hands dirty and create things during our events like the Microsoft Machine Learning Challenge or CGI’s Imagine Challenge; however, you also get to learn from the people in the trenches--entrepreneurs, pioneers in technology and the guys who build and maintain the tech we use everyday.

Is a schedule available?

The schedule will be up very soon. We’re currently crafting something that gives you the best experience possible.

What am I getting for my $$?

We’ve been working hard to cut prices as much as possible to make sure cost doesn’t get in your way. Conference tickets cost $30, which gets you full access to events like Bobby Motta’s illusion show (this alone would normally cost you $35), CGI’s Imagine Challenge and Microsoft’s Machine Learning Challenge. We’ll also be providing scrumptious breakfasts, lunches, dinners and snacks on both days to keep you going.

Where can I stay during the conference?

You can find lots of affordable accommodation around the Toronto area such as the Courtyard Marriot, but you can also try AirBnB, CouchSurfers, or even your friends! We're working out a deal with a nearby hotel - we'll keep you posted on that!

Will there be conference subsidies?

Yes! We'll be realeasing more information very soon.

I can’t attend anymore, can I get a refund?

CUTC will not be able to offer refunds to delegates unless they are under extenuating circumstances. If that is the case, please e-mail info@cutc.ca. Otherwise, we do extend the option of transferring your ticket to another person.

Can I transfer my ticket to another person?

Absolutely, just shoot us an e-mail and we’ll send that person a link to fill out a new registration form so they don’t have to use your name tag.

I’m interested in volunteering for CUTC, how can I get involved?

Applications for volunteers will be released in late March. Watch out on our Facebook and Twitter for more updates!

I still have questions!

Send 'em our way at info@cutc.ca.
