ABOUT

What is CUTC Imagine?

CUTC (Canadian Undergraduate Technology Conference) is Canada's largest student run undergraduate conference. Today, technology is evolving faster than mankind. For future generations to stay relevant, we must solve today’s biggest challenges - from advancing global healthcare & spreading education to protecting the environment & growing our economies. At CUTC 2016, we want to inspire the leaders of tomorrow (you) to solve problems that matter - to imagine a better future.

Why Should I come?

CUTC brings together more than 450 students and industry experts together for a weekend of talks, hacking and learning. CUTC is not like any other conference - we're breaking away from typical conference structure to give you the latest and greatest with tech expos, breakout sessions and competitions so you can learn, hack and maybe even leave with a some extra swag and cash prizes.

Who's It For?

Are you a student? Are you passionate about the future of technology and the impact it will have? Then CUTC Imagine is for you! This year we’re opening up the floor to include high school and graduate students as well.

EVENTS

Tech Expo

The exposition enables you to interact with companies and organizations to experience firsthand the future of technology through demos of gadgets and innovations in hardware, robotics, wearables, virtual reality, IoT and more. Previous participants include Microsoft, BlackBerry, Bell, the world-record holding University of Waterloo Solar Car team and others.

The Imagine Pitch Competition

Pitch your idea or startup at the conference to industry judges and successful entrepreneurs to compete for mentorship, office hours and prizes.

Mini-Challenges

These short competitions allow you to learn by doing. With multiple topic options from Security and Coding to Design and Product, they’re the perfect way to impress employers looking for their next all-star employee.

Product Vision Challenge

You will be challenged to solve real-world problems with technology through this unique case competition. Work together with others before the conference using a human-centered design approach to propose your ideas as real world products.

Office Hours

An exclusive opportunity if you're working on a project or company to get mentorship from accelerators, entrepreneurs and industry leaders.

Maker Fair

A forum for the maker in you to showcase your projects, companies and stories to inspire your peers to start building.
