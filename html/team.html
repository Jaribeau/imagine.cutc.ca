TEAM

The CUTC Team is comprised of immensely passionate individuals
who love technology, innovation, disruption, entrepreneurship, and
above all else, the expansion of the human potential through
invention. We're a team of young leaders within the startup and tech
ecosystem of Canada. We've been around for almost 2 decades and have
served over 500 students annually. The CUTC Team is ultiamtely a family
of technology afficionados and we would absolutely love to share that with you!
